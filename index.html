Research

Working Papers

  • The Regional Keynesian Cross, with Adrien Couturier and Rustam Jamilov
    Paper Data BibTeX Revise & Resubmit, Review of Economic Studies

    We study regional propagation of fiscal shocks in a heterogeneous-economy framework and establish conditions for a “regional Keynesian cross.” Using newly assembled data, we estimate multipliers that vary with local slack, openness, and fiscal space.

  • The Sentiment Channel of Fiscal Policy, with Adrien Couturier and Jacopo Tozzo
    Paper BibTeX

    We document a sentiment-driven transmission channel of fiscal policy. Exploiting high-frequency news and survey data, we show that policy announcements shift expectations and real outcomes beyond standard income and substitution effects.

  • A Distributional Theory of Household Sentiment, with Adrien Couturier and Seyed Hosseini Maasoum
    Paper Slides BibTeX

    We develop a distributional model of household sentiment in which heterogeneity in income, wealth, and exposure to shocks generates distinct expectation dynamics and aggregate implications.
